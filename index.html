What is SBA?

SBA — Singular BioArt

Where rarity meets digital immortality

Welcome to the pinnacle of digital ownership. SBA is not an NFT — it is a curated, authenticated artifact that exists only once in the world. Every SBA creation is born through a protected AI process, recorded in the Official Registry of Authenticity, and shielded by digital law.

The Essence of SBA

  • Absolute Singularity — only one original copy exists globally. No duplicates. No reproductions. No variants.
  • Impenetrable Scarcity — maximum of 5 artifacts per month per collector. Desire for more does not guarantee access.
  • Certified Creation — each artifact is registered and verified by SBA before entering the Official Registry. Without this, the piece holds no legal recognition and is deemed null.
  • Unique Identity — every SBA receives a Unique ID; upon creation, you are issued an Ownership Certificate stating that this SBA belongs exclusively to you.
  • Permanent Record — your nickname and ownership data are inscribed into the Official SBA Database, forever linking the artifact to its rightful owner.

Participation Privileges

  • Collector’s Membership: $30/month → up to 5 authenticated artifacts.
  • Exhibition Listing: $10 per artifact to present it in the SBA Market Hall.
  • Initiation Tier: First artifact creation is complimentary; market listing remains $10.
