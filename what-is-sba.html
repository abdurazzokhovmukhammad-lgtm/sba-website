What is an SBA?

SBA — Singular BioArt

SBA is born from half of your idea and half of artificial intelligence. Each SBA is immutable and unique — only one exists in the world.

The essence of an SBA

  • Absolute Singularity — only a single original copy exists globally; no duplicates or variants.
  • Unique ID — every SBA receives a unique digital identifier (from 0 to ∞). Lower numbers are rarer and typically more desirable.
  • Immutable — the artwork and its registration cannot be altered after certification.
